Vision

GM School of Law will have a transformative impact on society through continual innovation in legal education, research, skill development, creativity, and entrepreneurship.

Mission

  • To disseminate knowledge and conduct research in law with learner-centric approach.
  • To teach skills such as critical thinking, creativity and innovation, collaboration, communication, technical and digital, flexibility and adaptability, cultural values, and leadership and responsibility.
  • To develop global citizens by educating students on emotional, physical, social, economic, environmental, and spiritual dimensions of human growth in addition to intellectual pursuits.
  • To address real-world challenges and to establish the groundwork for entrepreneurship and lifelong learning.

Objectives

  • Deliver high-quality education in core and emerging areas of law to produce competent, ethical, and socially responsible legal professionals.
  • Promote advanced research and innovation in contemporary legal issues to address societal, industrial, and environmental challenges.
  • Foster interdisciplinary learning and collaboration between law, technology, management, and social sciences to develop well-rounded legal practitioners.
  • Develop professional, advocacy, and leadership skills among students to excel in diverse national and international legal environments.
  • Engage with judiciary, legal fraternity, industry, and community through legal aid, consultancy, skill development, and awareness programs for justice and social development.

About GM School of Law

Excellence in Legal Education, Research & Justice

The GM School of Law is committed to excellence in teaching, research, and professional development. Established with the vision of preparing students to address the evolving challenges of law and justice in a dynamic society, the department offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs that combine strong theoretical foundations with practical exposure.

Our curriculum covers diverse areas of law such as Constitutional Law, Administrative Law, Criminal Law, Civil Law, Corporate and Commercial Law, International Law, Intellectual Property Rights, Human Rights, Environmental Law, Cyber Law, and Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR). Students are encouraged to build critical thinking, advocacy skills, and ethical values through moot courts, legal aid clinics, internships, debates, seminars, and research projects.

The department is supported by a team of highly qualified faculty members engaged in teaching, research, consultancy, and policy studies. Equipped moot court halls, digital legal databases, and access to journals foster an enriching academic environment.

Collaborations with the Bar, Bench, law firms, NGOs, and corporate legal departments provide excellent placement and career opportunities. Graduates excel in litigation, judiciary, corporate practice, legal consultancy, academia, and public service.

The GM School of Law aims to promote justice, social responsibility, and the rule of law through education and professional engagement.

Mr. Shivakumargouda Patil

Director, School of Law
GM University, Davanagere

Programs Offered

Shaping Legal Minds with Academic Excellence & Ethical Foundation

LL.B. (Bachelor of Laws)

Duration: 3 Years

A 3-year program for graduates from any discipline such as B.A., B.Sc., B.Com., B.B.A., B.E., or equivalent. The program focuses on core legal principles, case analysis, advocacy, and professional ethics.

B.B.A., LL.B.

Duration: 5 Years

A 5-year integrated program for students who have completed PUC (10+2) in any stream. Combines legal studies with business administration to prepare graduates for corporate and legal management roles.

B.Com., LL.B.

Duration: 5 Years

A 5-year integrated course for students who have completed PUC (10+2) in any stream. Offers a blend of commerce and law subjects, ideal for careers in corporate law, taxation, and financial regulations.
